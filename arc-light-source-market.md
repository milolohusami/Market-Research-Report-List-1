<p><p>The Arc Light Source market includes various types of Xenon lamps designed for specific applications. Continuous-output Xenon short-arc lamps deliver stable, high-intensity light for projections and cinematography, making them ideal for short-range applications. Continuous-output Xenon long-arc lamps produce a more extensive and intense light beam, suitable for larger installations and professional theaters. Xenon flash lamps, on the other hand, emit brief, powerful bursts of light, often used in photography and scientific applications for their high luminance and rapid response.</p></p>
