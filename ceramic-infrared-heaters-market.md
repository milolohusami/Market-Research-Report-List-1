<p><p>Ceramic infrared heaters are categorized into three market types based on their wavelength emissions: short wave, medium wave, and long wave. Short wave heaters emit high-intensity infrared radiation, producing quick heat ideal for direct heating applications. Medium wave heaters provide balanced heating, suitable for both direct and ambient warmth, often used in industrial settings. Long wave heaters offer lower intensity, providing gentle and even heating, making them ideal for larger areas and applications requiring consistent warmth over time.</p></p>
