<p><p>The consumer batteries market comprises various types catering to different needs. Nickel Cadmium (NiCad) batteries offer durability and rechargeability but have lower energy density. Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) provides higher capacity and is more environmentally friendly. Alkaline batteries are disposable, widely used for household devices, but not rechargeable. Primary Lithium batteries offer long life and high energy output for critical applications. Lithium-ion (Li-Ion) batteries are rechargeable, lightweight, and prevalent in portable electronics, electric vehicles, and renewable energy storage.</p></p>
