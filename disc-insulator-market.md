<p><p>The disc insulator market encompasses three primary types: glass, porcelain, and polymer insulators. Glass insulators are known for their superior dielectric properties and durability, often used in high-voltage applications. Porcelain insulators offer excellent mechanical strength and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for diverse conditions. Polymer insulators are lightweight, flexible, and resistant to pollution, increasingly popular in modern power systems. Each type serves specific electrical insulation needs, balancing performance, durability, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
