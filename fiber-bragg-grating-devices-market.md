<p><strong>The Fiber Bragg Grating Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evenly</li><li>Inhomogeneous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Bragg Grating (FBG) devices are categorized into two main market types: evenly distributed and inhomogeneous. Evenly distributed FBGs feature consistent grating intervals along the optical fiber, providing uniform wavelength response and typically used in applications like temperature and strain sensing. In contrast, inhomogeneous FBGs have varying grating characteristics, allowing for more complex wavelength responses, which are beneficial for multiplexing signals and advanced sensing applications. Each market type caters to diverse industry needs, enhancing the functionality of optical systems.</p></p>
