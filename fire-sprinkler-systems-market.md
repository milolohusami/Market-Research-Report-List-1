<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fire Sprinkler Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Pipe Fire Sprinkler Systems</li><li>Dry Pipe Fire Sprinkler Systems</li><li>Deluge Fire Sprinkler Systems</li><li>Pre-Action Fire Sprinkler Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire sprinkler systems are essential for fire protection in various settings. Wet pipe systems are the most common, featuring pressurized water in pipes, activating upon fire detection. Dry pipe systems use pressurized air to keep water in reserve, suitable for unheated spaces. Deluge systems have open sprinkler heads, releasing large water volumes quickly for high-hazard areas. Pre-action systems combine elements of wet and dry systems, requiring two triggers before activating, making them ideal for sensitive environments like data centers.</p></p>
