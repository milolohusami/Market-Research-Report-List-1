<p><ul><li>Glass Type Sensor</li><li>ISFET Sensor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory pH sensor market is primarily categorized into three types: glass type sensors, ISFET sensors, and others. Glass type sensors comprise a glass membrane that reacts to hydrogen ion concentration, providing accurate pH readings. ISFET (Ion-Sensitive Field-Effect Transistor) sensors utilize semiconductor technology for more compact and durable designs, ideal for miniaturized applications. "Others" include various innovative pH sensing technologies, such as optical and polymer sensors, offering alternative solutions for specific research and industrial needs.</p></p>
