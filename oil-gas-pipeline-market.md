<p><p>The oil and gas pipeline market encompasses various types of pipes, each with distinct characteristics. Electric Resistance Welding (ERW) Steel Pipes, known for their cost-effectiveness, are suitable for low-pressure applications. Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) Steel Pipes offer high strength and are used in large-diameter projects. Seamless Steel Pipes provide superior strength and reliability for high-pressure environments. Polyethylene and Composite Pipes, resistant to corrosion and lightweight, are increasingly utilized in modern applications, enhancing flexibility and efficiency in pipeline installations.</p></p>
