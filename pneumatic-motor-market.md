<p><strong>The Pneumatic Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vane Air Motor</li><li>Piston Air Motor</li><li>Gear Air Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic motor market comprises several types, primarily including vane air motors, piston air motors, and gear air motors. Vane air motors utilize sliding vanes to convert compressed air into mechanical power, offering high torque at low speeds. Piston air motors leverage cylinders and pistons for a more direct conversion of air pressure into rotational motion, providing high efficiency. Gear air motors use gears to reduce speed while increasing torque, making them suitable for applications requiring consistent power output. Together, these motor types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
