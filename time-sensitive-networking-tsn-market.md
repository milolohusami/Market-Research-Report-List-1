<p><p>Time-Sensitive Networking (TSN) enhances Ethernet for real-time applications by providing deterministic data delivery. Key standards include IEEE 802.1AS for time synchronization, IEEE 802.1Qca for path control, IEEE 802.1AS-Rev for improved timing functions, IEEE 802.1Qbv for scheduled traffic, IEEE 802.1Qci for frame filtering, IEEE 802.1CB for seamless packet replication, IEEE 802.1Qcc for stream reservation, IEEE 802.1Qch for channel-efficient bandwidth, and IEEE 802.1CM for audio-video bridging. Together, they ensure reliable and low-latency communication in industrial and multimedia settings.</p></p>
